Description:

This visionary project aims to develop an innovative website that provides real-time monitoring and analysis of critical water parameters essential for successful aquaculture. By harnessing cutting-edge technologies such as Next.js, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and PHP, we are committed to ensuring optimal conditions for aquatic life, improving sustainability, and enhancing the efficiency of aquaculture practices.

Note: The project is currently at a confidential stage. For further information or inquiries, please contact the Project Manager, Dr. Thien Nguyen, at thien.nguyen@torrens.edu.au.


Our Web-Based Solution:

Our pioneering web platform will empower administrators and users to effortlessly access and monitor crucial water parameters, including pH levels, H2S concentration, NO3 levels, NH4 concentration, and temperature (T0). This comprehensive system will seamlessly integrate with live data from a centralized server, offering a user-friendly interface for efficient and reliable water quality management.

By combining state-of-the-art technology with the wisdom of aquaculture experts, we are poised to revolutionize water management in aquaculture, ultimately contributing to the prosperity of Binh Dinh Province's aquaculture industry.
